Types of Guns

There are different types of guns, including rifles, shotguns, and pistols. Each type serves different purposes, from hunting to self-defense to military usage.

Rifles

Rifles are typically used for longer-range shooting, such as with sniper rifles or hunting rifles.

Shotguns

Shotguns are often used for close-range combat and hunting birds or small game.
